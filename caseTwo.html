Golfhandelen.no

Re-Designing Website

For this current project I am re-designing Golfhandelen.no to a more modern e-commerce golf website. I conducted a comparative analysis where I listed positive and the negative features of other golf websites. With this information I could gather features and looks that I wanted to implement to the new website. However, being introduced to Golfhandelens e-commerce program: Askås, we quickly realized we were limited with the visual design and the UI-design that was created. Although we were limited, we tried our best to implement some parts of the UI-design.

It was a learning curve that it is not always possible that the design will turn out as you would want it to. What is important though, is that our customer was happy with the design and made it easier for their customers to buy their equipments. I was and am also in charge of re-making their banners (for website and also Facebook page) and visual aspects to fit their new design, using Illustrator and Photoshop.
