Hue Cuisine

Fictive Project

  • Figma
  • UX/UI
  • Hi-fi Prototype

Background

A food delivery application for a fictive vietnamese restaurant called Hue Cuisine. Main focus is for the restaurant to provide their menu on the application for customers to order. Based on a persona that wants an easy application to use, with some features in mind. Key features for the persona that is in the application: filtering option, search bar, being able to follow order time and showcase of the menu.

Details

Customers will be able to get an overview of the menu with detailed description, price and possible allergens. Working with color contrasts, I focused on bold colors but also a light color to resemble the Vietnamese flag (red and yellow).
