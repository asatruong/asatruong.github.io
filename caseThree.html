Background

A school project made in Wordpress in 2022 for a Swedish lamp fixtures company. My peer and I re-designed this website and opted for a user-friendly design. By conducting interviews and observations with users we gathered insightful information about what could be improved with the website. A persona was created and lo-fi and hi-fi prototypes was created before the implementation of the website.

Details

We started with contacting potential companies that would be interested in a re-design of their website. We got hold of a small lamp fixtures company that wanted some freshen up! In the process we got to have frequent meetings on Zoom, and constantly e-mailing back and forth.

Lessons

I remember one of the lessons I learnt while doing this project back in 2020, that you had to have lots of patience as my peer and I encountered a language barrier with the business owner. It caused some misunderstandings between the parties, but in the end we managed to create a design and text writing that he liked!
